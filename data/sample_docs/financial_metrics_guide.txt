TechCorp Inc. - Financial Metrics and KPI Guide

Introduction:
This document provides definitions and context for key financial metrics used in TechCorp's reporting.

Revenue Metrics:

1. Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
   Definition: Annualized value of recurring subscription contracts
   Calculation: Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) × 12
   Importance: Predictability of future revenue streams
   TechCorp Target: 20% YoY growth

2. Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
   Definition: Percentage of recurring revenue retained from existing customers, including expansions
   Calculation: (Starting ARR + Expansion - Churn) / Starting ARR × 100
   Best-in-Class: >120%
   TechCorp Performance: 115-118%

3. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
   Definition: Total cost to acquire a new customer
   Calculation: (Sales + Marketing Expenses) / Number of New Customers
   TechCorp Average: $45,000
   Industry Benchmark: $35,000-$60,000

4. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
   Definition: Total revenue expected from a customer over their lifetime
   Calculation: (Average Revenue per Customer × Gross Margin) / Churn Rate
   TechCorp Average: $250,000
   Target LTV/CAC Ratio: >3.0x

5. Average Contract Value (ACV)
   Definition: Average annualized revenue per customer contract
   Enterprise Segment: $125,000
   Mid-Market Segment: $45,000
   SMB Segment: $12,000

Profitability Metrics:

1. Gross Margin
   Definition: Revenue minus cost of goods sold, expressed as percentage
   Calculation: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100
   TechCorp Target: 68-70%
   SaaS Industry Average: 70-80%

2. Operating Margin
   Definition: Operating income as a percentage of revenue
   Calculation: Operating Income / Revenue × 100
   TechCorp Target: 17-20%
   Path to 25% by 2026

3. EBITDA Margin
   Definition: Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization
   TechCorp Performance: 27-28%
   Public SaaS Median: 20-25%

4. Free Cash Flow (FCF)
   Definition: Cash generated after capital expenditures
   Calculation: Operating Cash Flow - Capital Expenditures
   TechCorp FY2023: $280 million
   FCF Margin: 17%

Efficiency Metrics:

1. Rule of 40
   Definition: Sum of revenue growth rate and profit margin
   Calculation: Revenue Growth % + Operating Margin %
   Benchmark: >40% is excellent
   TechCorp Score: 35% (15% growth + 20% margin)

2. Magic Number
   Definition: Sales efficiency metric
   Calculation: Net New ARR / Prior Quarter Sales & Marketing Spend
   Interpretation: >0.75 is efficient
   TechCorp Performance: 0.85

3. Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)
   Definition: Percentage of revenue retained excluding expansions
   Calculation: (Starting ARR - Churned ARR) / Starting ARR × 100
   TechCorp Performance: 95%
   Best-in-Class: >95%

4. Burn Multiple
   Definition: How much capital burned to generate $1 of ARR growth
   Calculation: Net Burn / Net New ARR
   Good: <1.5x
   TechCorp: 0.8x (efficient)

Operational Metrics:

1. Monthly Active Users (MAU)
   Platform Users: 2.5 million
   Growth Rate: 12% MoM
   Engagement Rate: 68%

2. Net Promoter Score (NPS)
   Definition: Customer satisfaction and loyalty metric
   TechCorp Score: 68
   Industry Average: 30-40
   Calculation: % Promoters - % Detractors

3. Customer Health Score
   Components: Product usage, support tickets, payment history, engagement
   Green (Healthy): 75% of customers
   Yellow (At Risk): 20% of customers
   Red (Churning): 5% of customers

4. Time to Value (TTV)
   Definition: Time from purchase to realizing value
   Enterprise: 45 days average
   Mid-Market: 30 days average
   SMB: 14 days average

Balance Sheet Metrics:

1. Current Ratio
   Definition: Current assets divided by current liabilities
   TechCorp: 2.8
   Healthy Ratio: >2.0

2. Debt-to-Equity Ratio
   Definition: Total debt divided by shareholders' equity
   TechCorp: 0.25
   Conservative Leverage: <0.5

3. Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)
   Definition: Average days to collect payment
   TechCorp: 45 days
   Industry Standard: 30-60 days

4. Cash Conversion Cycle
   Definition: Time to convert investments into cash flows
   Components: DSO + Days Inventory Outstanding - Days Payable Outstanding
   TechCorp: 35 days
